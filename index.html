Who am I ?

I'm a 15 years old French Developper. I mostly develop with Java and C/C++ on Eclipse or CodeBlocks and I'm a Linux lover <3 (but I also use Windows, especially for gaming).

I create some basic apps, some mini-games and also some Bukkit/Spigot plugins (for Minecraft).

You can see a list of all my knew programming languages in the experience section.
